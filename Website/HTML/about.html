Unnamed bot - About

Information about the bot and what it's for

UnNamed is an advanced and deeply customizable Moderation and Economy bot, with a simple Web Dashboard and extensive, useful and fun commands. The bot has cross saving so you can share your work replies with friends! Started in July 2020 and released in XYZ 2020, the bot was coded in node.js (discord.js) and python, and the website in HTML. It is currently in Alpha, but why not give it a try!
